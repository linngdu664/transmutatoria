# Essence Metals

*The Sun is its father, the Moon its mother; the wind carried it in its womb, the earth nourished it;*

*Here is the source of every perfection in the world, and its power is obtained from the earth;*

## Overview

In the previous chapter, you obtained your first batch of essence metals. This chapter looks deeper into the relationships between them - restraint and symbiosis are the core logic of Ars Transmutatoria, running through all later reactions.

## Restraint and Symbiosis

### Four-Pillar Restraint

Review the twelve metals and their four-pillar affiliations from the previous chapter:

| Four Pillars | Essence Metals |
|------|---------|
| Starfire | Eclipsium, Lunargent, Astrotite |
| Abyssal Water | Abyssion, Animercury, Necroplumb |
| Rotten Earth | Sanguibronze, Venotite, Ossantimony |
| Calamitous Wind | Fulgurzinc, Chronoplatinum, Pandemonium |

There is a fixed restraint cycle between the pillars:

**Starfire -> Calamitous Wind -> Rotten Earth -> Abyssal Water -> Starfire**

When two metals react, the restraining side activates (state increases), and the restrained side deteriorates (state decreases). For example, when Animercury of the Abyssal Water pillar meets Eclipsium of the Starfire pillar, Animercury activates and Eclipsium deteriorates.

### Elemental Restraint

On top of the restraint between pillars, some metals also have additional restraint relationships. After the two layers are combined, a relationship that originally did not restrain may become restraint, **double restraint** may appear, and even **mutual restraint** may form.

### Symbiosis

Some metals have a **symbiosis** relationship, and both sides activate when reacting. For example, Lunargent and Venotite have symbiosis.

### Pandemonium

Pandemonium is a special exception: **it restrains all other 11 essences and is not restrained by any essence**. It is not bound by the four-pillar relationships and is a key material for advanced alchemy.

The above are the core rules of restraint and symbiosis. For the complete table of restraint and symbiosis relationships, see [Appendix: Essence Relations].

## Emerald Tablet

Crafted from 1 Transmutation Crystal and 4 emeralds in a crafting table.

Right-click while holding the Emerald Tablet to check the complete restraint relationship between any two essence metals. When you need to confirm the result of a reaction, it is the most reliable reference tool.

## Essence States

Every essence metal has four states:

| Name | State Value | Description |
|---|---|------|
| Nigredo | -1 | Deteriorated by one stage |
| Base | 0 | Base form; essences freshly decomposed are all base state |
| Albedo | 1 | Activated by one stage |
| Citrinitas | 2 | Activated by two stages |

After the state exceeds the range, the metal is destroyed: **below -1 becomes Alchemical Dross**, and **above +2 becomes redstone dust**. Both lose their alchemical use.

The state of an essence does not affect its nature - Nigredo-tainted Eclipsium and Citrinitas-infused Eclipsium have exactly the same restraint relationships and potency.

## Alchemist's Storage Box

The storage box is the core tool for managing essence stock. It is both an item and a placeable block.

**Crafting and variants**: The storage box has four variants - normal, Nigredo, Albedo, and Citrinitas - which respectively store only essence metals of the corresponding state. Each storage box provides 12 slots, one for each metal.

**Automatic storage**: When you pick up essence metals, a storage box in your inventory with the matching state will automatically absorb them.

**Use while held**: Right-click while holding a storage box to open it and view or organize stored essences. When holding a storage box and looking at a crucible, an essence wheel appears above the HUD for convenient feeding.

::: tip Tip
The storage box is a block form when placed on the ground. Shift + right-click to retrieve it. See [Appendix: Alchemist's Storage Box] for details.
:::

After mastering essence relationships, states, and storage methods, the next chapter will teach you how to use the crucible for actual essence reactions.
