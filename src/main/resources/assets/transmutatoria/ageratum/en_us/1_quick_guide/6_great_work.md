# The Great Work

*This is complete; I testify to it by the judgment of the world;*

*This is complete, by the miracle of almighty power;*

## Overview

The Great Work is the ultimate goal of alchemy - the refinement of the **Philosopher's Stone**. The whole process is the ultimate test of all your arts: first prepare the three matrices as scroll materials for Alchemical Transformation, then pass through the four stages of Nigredo -> Albedo -> Citrinitas -> Rubedo, and finally calcine Rubedo Matter to obtain the Philosopher's Stone.

## Three Matrices

Ancient alchemy holds that all things are composed of the three matrices of Salt, Mercury, and Sulfur. In the Great Work, you need to produce them in sequence through Alchemical Transformation as scroll materials for later stages:

| Matrix | Transformation Input | Level | Polarity Requirement |
|------|------|------|----------|
| Salic Matrix | Sand | 4 | <= 0 |
| Mercurial Matrix | Salic Matrix | 8 | >= 20 |
| Sulfuric Matrix | Mercurial Matrix | 13 | -1 to 1 |

Note that Sulfuric Matrix requires polarity between -1 and 1 - the window is extremely narrow and requires precise control.

## Prima Materia

Use 1 Transmutation Crystal and 4 cobblestone in a crafting table to craft **Prima Materia**. It is the starting material for the four stages of the Great Work.

## Four Stages

### Nigredo

> *"That which is below is like that which is above, and that which is above is like that which is below"*

Matter is decomposed in darkness and returns to chaos.

| Condition | Value |
|------|-----|
| Transformation Input | Prima Materia |
| Product | Nigredo Matter |
| Level | 3 |
| Polarity Requirement | **-50 to -10** |

The slots are few and the operation is simple, but it requires the crucible polarity to be negative.

### Albedo

> *"The earth nourished it; the wind carried it in its womb"*

Darkness is washed clean, and matter is purified into silver-white light.

| Condition | Value |
|------|-----|
| Transformation Input | Nigredo Matter |
| Product | Albedo Matter |
| Level | 6 |
| Polarity Requirement | **20 to 50** |

Polarity reverses - after the Nigredo stage, you need to greatly raise polarity to a positive value.

### Citrinitas

> *"The Sun is its father, the Moon its mother"*

The silver-white light is dyed golden by the sun, and matter gains the power of transformation.

| Condition | Value |
|------|-----|
| Transformation Input | Albedo Matter |
| Product | Citrinitas Matter |
| Level | 12 |
| Polarity Requirement | **40 to 50** |

The slot count doubles to 12, special slots begin to appear, and the polarity window is narrow and high.

### Rubedo

> *"This is the force of all forces and the strength of all strength"*

The gold is ignited into crimson, and the embryo of the Philosopher's Stone is born here. This is a one-time transformation.

| Condition | Value |
|------|-----|
| Transformation Input | Citrinitas Matter |
| Product | Rubedo Matter |
| Level | **24** |
| Polarity Requirement | **49 to 50** |
| One-Time | **Yes** |

This is the most difficult step in the entire Great Work: 24 slots, about a 50% chance for special slots; a polarity window with only 2 points of tolerance. It is recommended to use Lunar or higher-tier scrolls, prepare ample reserves of essence metals, and stabilize polarity at 49 to 50 before beginning.

### Calcination

Smelt Rubedo Matter in a **furnace** to obtain the Philosopher's Stone.

## Powers of the Philosopher's Stone

The Philosopher's Stone is the supreme achievement of alchemy. Holding it grants the following abilities:

**Life protection**: When held in the main hand or offhand, continuously grants regeneration. Every second grants a brief saturation effect, removing the need to eat. If an off-cooldown Philosopher's Stone exists in the hotbar when fatal damage occurs, the death is canceled - health is locked to 1 point, and that stone enters a **60-second cooldown**.

**Healing corruption**: Right-click a zombie villager while holding the Philosopher's Stone to complete conversion directly, without the vanilla weakness potion and golden apple process.

**Polarity adjustment**: Right-click the Transmutation Crucible while holding the Philosopher's Stone to adjust the crucible's polarity back toward 0 by **1 point**.

**Chaos Decomposition**: The Philosopher's Stone can be put into the crucible as a catalyst to reverse-decompose items that can be replicated by Sigil Scrolls into essence metals. This is an effective way to obtain large amounts of essence.
