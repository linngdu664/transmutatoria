# Ars Transmutatoria

![](transmutatoria:item/liber_transmutationis_ars_transmutatoria.png)

Ars Transmutatoria is an alchemy mod about changing matter with water, catalysts, essence metals, and carefully managed polarity.

## First Steps

1. Craft and place a **Transmutation Crucible**.
2. Fill it with water. Each reaction consumes a small amount.
3. Add a catalyst, then provide the matching input items or essence metals.
4. Watch the crucible polarity. Too much imbalance can turn the crucible into an unwanted block.

<recipe id="transmutatoria:transmutation_crucible"/>

## Core Tools

- **Transmutation Crucible**: the main alchemical workstation.
- **Essence Metals**: twelve metals with four states and relation-driven reactions.
- **Transmutation Scrolls**: activate a replication or transformation pattern, then satisfy its alchemy slots in the crucible.
- **Alchemist's Storage Box**: stores one state of every essence metal and can feed the crucible quickly.
- **Philosopher's Stone**: protects its bearer and can nudge crucible polarity back toward zero.
