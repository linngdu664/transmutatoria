# Liber Transmutationis: Ars Transmutatoria

![Logo](../textures/logo.png)

## Introduction

The world is borne by four pillars: **Starfire, Abyssal Water, Rotten Earth, and Calamitous Wind**. Each pillar divides into three essence metals, twelve in all. They nourish, restrain, or annihilate one another - this is the foundation of Ars Transmutatoria.

Dissolve redstone, gunpowder, and glowstone in water, catalyzed by emerald, to obtain a Transmutation Crystal. With this as your beginning, craft a Transmutation Crucible, decompose essence metals from it, and you will gradually master Alchemical Replication, Alchemical Transformation, and even the refinement of the Philosopher's Stone.

May the wisdom of the Emerald Tablet guide your path.

# Alchemist's Manual

This manual is arranged according to the play flow. It guides you from obtaining your first Transmutation Crystal to gradually mastering every craft of Ars Transmutatoria. Each chapter focuses on one core activity; read one chapter and practice one chapter, and you can progress naturally.

## Chapter 1: [Getting Started](1_introduction.md)

Craft a Transmutation Crystal and Transmutation Crucible, obtain your first batch of essence metals through Transmutation Decomposition, and first meet the twelve metals.

## Chapter 2: [Essence Metals](2_essence_metals.md)

Understand the four-pillar affiliations and restraint relationships of the twelve essence metals, learn to use the Emerald Tablet to check restraint relations, recognize the four states of essence, and organize your stock with storage boxes.

## Chapter 3: [Basic Alchemy](3_basic_alchemy.md)

Master the basic operation of the crucible. Use a Transmutation Crystal to catalyze Essence Reaction and change metal states; use essence metals to catalyze Essence Fusion and synthesize essence metals. Introduces the concept of polarity.

## Chapter 4: [Advanced Alchemy - Part I](4_advanced_alchemy_1.md)

Craft transmutation scrolls, distinguish Sigil Scrolls (Alchemical Replication) from Equation Scrolls (Alchemical Transformation). Activate a scroll, complete your first scroll alchemy in the crucible, and understand slot matching and annihilation.

## Chapter 5: [Advanced Alchemy - Part II](5_advanced_alchemy_2.md)

Go deeper into the details of scroll alchemy: how entropy accumulates and accelerates scroll wear, how the polarity window affects output, scroll expiration and mutation, and the basic concepts of advanced slots.

## Chapter 6: [The Great Work](6_great_work.md)

Refine the three matrices of Salt, Mercury, and Sulfur, pass through the four stages of Nigredo -> Albedo -> Citrinitas -> Rubedo, and finally calcine Rubedo Matter to obtain the supreme achievement of alchemy - the Philosopher's Stone.
